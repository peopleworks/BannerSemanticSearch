BC027|Payroll|PHRHIST vs PERETOT — When to Use Each|PHRHIST,PERETOT,PHRDEDN|Multiple|PHRHIST (Payroll History) stores individual payroll detail records with sequence numbers (SEQ_NO 0=original, 1=reversal, 2=correction).
