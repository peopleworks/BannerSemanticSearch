SC002|Security|GURUOBJ role suffix interpretation|GURUOBJ|guruobj_role|The LAST CHARACTER of guruobj_role usually indicates access level: Q=Query (read-only), M=Maintenance (read/write), B=Both, U=Update-only. At WCC the distribution is M=76%, Q=24%. BUT there are custom Banner roles where the last char is NOT the suffix: BAN_DEFAULT_NO_ACCESS (S), BAN_DEFAULT_CONNECT (T), BAN_DEFAULT_CMQUERYEXECUTE_HR (R). So SUBSTR(guruobj_role,-1) is a 99.97%-correct shortcut but fails on custom roles.|Don't blindly SUBSTR(role,-1).
