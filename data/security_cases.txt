SC002|Security|GURUOBJ role suffix interpretation|GURUOBJ|guruobj_role|The LAST CHARACTER of guruobj_role usually indicates access level: Q=Query (read-only), M=Maintenance (read/write), B=Both, U=Update-only. So SUBSTR(guruobj_role,-1) is a 99.97%-correct shortcut but fails on custom roles.|Don't blindly SUBSTR(role,-1).
