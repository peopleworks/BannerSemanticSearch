⚙ How It Works
BM25 Ranking: The same algorithm used by Elasticsearch and Apache Lucene. Ranks results by relevance considering term frequency and document length.

Banner Synonyms: Automatically expands your query with Banner abbreviations. "employee" also searches for EMPL, EMP, PEBEMPL.

Fuzzy Matching: Uses trigram similarity to handle typos and partial matches.

Column Patterns: Recognizes common Banner column types: _PIDM (Person ID), _CODE (Validation), _IND (Flag), _DATE, _AMT (Amount), _SURROGATE_ID (Key).
